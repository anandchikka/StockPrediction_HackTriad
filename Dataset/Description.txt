**Description about the Dataset**
  This dataset is the combination of 7 columns: 
    1. Date
    2. Open
    3. High
    4. Low
    5. Close
    6. Adj Close
    7. Volume
  This dataset is a table real world records of stock values on the particular date.
  The dataset is comprising of the prices from 14-03-1986 to 12-03-2015. 
  The dataset is retrieved from Microsoft Corporation (MFST)
